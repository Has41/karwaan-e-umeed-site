Together Against Child Labor

Karwaan-e-Umeed is on a mission to raise awareness, support victims, and eliminate child labor from society.

Our Impact Pillars

Why Does Child Labor Exist?

Child labor persists due to extreme poverty, lack of quality education, and weak enforcement of labor laws. Many families depend on the income of their children to survive, creating a vicious cycle of poverty and lost opportunities.

Learn More

What Do We Aim?

To combat child labor by identifying its root causes, raising awareness, and ensuring access to education for underprivileged children through community-driven initiatives, research-based advocacy, and sustainable partnerships.

Learn More

Why Would It Create an Impact?

Eliminating child labor not only protects children but also empowers communities. By ensuring children receive education instead of exploitation, we foster economic growth, reduce poverty, and create a future where every child can realize their potential.
